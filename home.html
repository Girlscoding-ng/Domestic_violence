Domestic Violence(HouseHelps)

Domestic violence,also known as domestic abuse or family violence is a type of violence or other abuse by one person against another in a domestic setting or cohabitation.

Domestic Violence on Househelps is the Mistreatment or Maltreatment on househelps. We observed that they undergo some or all sorts of Domestic Violence from their bosses. It is one of the under reported crimes happening in the world today, because some people believe that the househelps deserve to be maltreated and even the victims believe there is no help anywhere,thinking that their bosses have complete control over them and this leads to them being assaulted physically,sexually,psychologically and emotionally.

What we do

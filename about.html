Why This Programme?

It has been noticed that whenever people hear about Domestic Violence,they focus more on marital aspect,that is,Domestic Violence between husband and wife but,what of the househelp? We found out that they suffer more of Domestic Violence that the people in the family. This is the reason why we decided to create this website.

This website is created to end maltreatment of househelps through creating a means with which they can easily use to reach out for help and also to help people who are interested in domestic work in getting employed through a legal channel. Thereby, reducing maltreatment of Domestic workers.
